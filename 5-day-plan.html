The 5-Day Nicotine Hack

A 1-week immersive experience to hack your body, brain, and habits back from nicotine.

Day 1

The Body Hack

Morning Session

The Journey of Nicotine

Track nicotine from the lungs to the brain using interactive 3D models. Understand immediate physiological impacts.

Activity: "The Body Map" - Life-sized tracking of nicotine's path.
Afternoon Session

Heart Rate Challenge

Live bio-feedback. Measure heart rate changes before and after physical stress to simulate nicotine's load on the heart.

Activity: "The Heart Rate Circuit" - Physical challenges & monitoring.
Day 2

The Brain Hack

Morning Session

The Science of Addiction

How nicotine hijacks dopamine pathways. Gamified simulations of the reward system.

Activity: "The Brain Game" - Resource management & dopamine puzzles.
Afternoon Session

The Cost of Smoking

Financial impact analysis vs. personal goals. Budgeting games to visualize lost opportunities.

Activity: "The Budget Challenge" - Real-world financial hacking.
Day 3

The Social Hack

Morning Session

Media & Peer Pressure

Deconstructing tobacco marketing aimed at youth. Analyzing social media influence.

Activity: "Ad Busters" - Create counter-marketing campaigns.
Afternoon Session

Mastering Refusal Skills

Empowerment training. Role-playing high-pressure scenarios to build confidence.

Activity: "The Refusal Role-Play" - Interactive scenario simulations.
Day 4

The Health Hack

Morning Session

Benefits of a Smoke-Free Life

Visualizing the positive timeline of recovery. Lung capacity restoration and energy boosts.

Activity: "The Dream Board" - Visualizing future goals.
Afternoon Session

Healthy Alternatives

Replacing the habit loop. Exploration of sports, hobbies, and stress-relief techniques.

Activity: "Healthy Habits Fair" - Interactive stations for new hobbies.
Day 5

The Community Hack

Morning Session

Family Learning & Dialogue

Bridging the gap. Parents join for open dialogue and shared learning activities.

Activity: "The Family Pledge" - Commitment contracts.
Afternoon Session

The School Pledge

Culmination event. Celebrating commitments and the collective power of the student body.

Activity: "The School Pledge Assembly" - High-energy celebration.
