A Glimpse into the 5-Day Hack

A completely immersive week-long challenge to "hack" the nicotine habit.

Day 1

The Body Hack

Understanding the physiological takeover: Nicotine's journey from lungs to blood. Heart rate bio-feedback challenges.

Day 2

The Brain Hack

Dopamine hijacking and the science of addiction. Gamified simulations of how the brain gets "hacked".

Day 3

The Social Hack

Decoding the matrix: Analyzing media manipulation, peer pressure, and mastering the art of saying "No".

Day 4

The Health Hack

Reclaiming vitality: Visualizing recovery timelines and exploring high-energy healthy alternatives.

Day 5

The Community Hack

Solidifying the firewall: Family dialogues and a school-wide pledge to stay hack-proof and smoke-free.
